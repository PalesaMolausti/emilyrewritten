This is Emily Rewritten

"Designed with love & courage"

The Emily Rewritten Project is a faith-based non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals. Through storytelling, mentorship, and safe spaces, we help young people reclaim their narratives and find strength in their journeys.

Its roots—Latin aemulus meaning “rival” and Germanic amal meaning “industrious”—echo biblical themes of perseverance and striving. These meanings reflect virtues such as diligence and faithfulness found throughout scripture. Spiritually, Emily can symbolize ambition, resilience, and the grace to rise above challenges—qualities.

About the Journey

  • Rewrite your story, Reclaim your power.Emily Rewritten is more than a story - it's a movement of love, Faith, and transformation. Through every chapter, we embrace the power of rewritting life's narrative with grace and hope, By providing opportunities and resources for youth to heal, adapt and lead with purpose. Committed to empowering and transforming lives through storytelling, support, and community.
  • Stories have the power to transform. By connecting with mentors, sharing experiences, and participating in our initiatives, members of our community find hope and strength to rewrite their own stories.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: Believing in the inherent strength and potential of every individual.
  • Community: Building a supportive and inclusive community where individuals can connect, share their stories, and find encouragement.
  • Resilience: Promoting resilience by providing resources and opportunities for personal growth, healing, and transformation.
  • Faith: Embracing faith as a source of hope, guidance, and strength in the journey of rewriting one's story.
  • Authenticity: Encouraging individuals to be true to themselves and embrace their unique stories and experiences.
