Stories of Emily

"Called to strive for righteousness.

Emily is a symbolic figure representing every young woman who has faced trauma but found the strength to rewrite her story. The name comes from the founder’s first published book, Emily Rewritten, which sparked this movement.

This journey is about resilience, healing, and authorship—reminding us that no matter what we’ve been through, we can take back the pen and write a new ending.

“This is where I rewrote my life.”

Submit Your Story
