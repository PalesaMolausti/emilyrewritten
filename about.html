Who is Emily Rewritten?

“A movement of resilience, healing, and authorship.”

Emily is a symbolic figure representing every young woman who has faced trauma but found strength to rewrite her story. The name comes from the founder first published book Emily rewritten, which sparked the movement. It is about resilience, healing, and authorship.

Core

Mission

Empowering youth to reclaim their narratives and discover their inner strength through storytelling, career mentorship, and everyday in-demand skills. Through meaningful mentorship connections, they gain wisdom, inspiration, and tools to navigate life’s challenges. We ignite a sense of belonging, helping them take charge of their stories with purpose.

Valued

Vision

Inspiring youth to become purpose-driven leaders, equipped with the tools, knowledge, and resilience to take charge of their future. We guide them in shaping unique paths, making meaningful decisions, and leaving a positive impact. By fostering growth and self-awareness, we envision a generation prepared to lead with courage, creativity, and responsibility.

Melissa Yvonne Skhosule founded EmilyRewritten.org on January 31, 2020, in South Africa, as a youth-centered organisation empowering young people to reclaim their stories. With over 4,000 youth impacted across Africa through social media platforms, virtual events, and mentorship sessions, it creates safe spaces for growth and leadership through career advice, intensive support, and creative expression. The organisation offers storytelling workshops, advocacy campaigns, and youth-led initiatives that help young people stand strong and lead with purpose.

Founder

Melissa's Message

Emily Rewritten began with one story—my own. Today, it belongs to thousands of voices reclaiming their power. We exist so every young person can rise beyond with courage and authorship.
